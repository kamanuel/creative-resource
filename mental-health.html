Mental Health

Psychotherapy and psychiatry are available at the clinic. If a patient needs a behavioral health referral (counseling), place a referral to the social worker of the day (SWOD). If a patient needs psychiatric medication management, place a referral to psychiatry. 


A resource was created by a previous longitudinal student that summarizes all the types of mental health services that are available. It also gives descriptions of what differentiates each type of mental health provider. Hard copies of this resource can be found in the workroom.
